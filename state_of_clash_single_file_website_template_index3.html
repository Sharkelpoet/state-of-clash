Breaking News

Supercell Faces Backlash Over Hero Timers

Players report growing frustration over long hero upgrade times at higher Town Halls. Many demand shorter timers or the ability to use heroes during upgrades. Community polls show over 80% support a change.

The Report

State of Clash — August 2025

We gathered feedback from thousands of players across Reddit, Discord, and in-game chats to compile the top community concerns:

  • Excessive hero upgrade timers
  • Lack of Global Chat & recruiting features
  • Weak trap damage and irrelevant walls
  • Uninspiring Builder Base rewards

Opinion

Why Global Chat Should Return

Global Chat was more than nostalgia — it was a social hub that connected clans and players. A modernized, moderated version could balance safety with the community’s desire for interaction.
